Sushi rolls

Ingredients

  • nori rolls
  • cooked sushi rice
  • raw salmon, thinly sliced
  • cucumber, thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. Place nori square on plate
  2. Take a spoonful of cooked sushi rice on top of nori
  3. Spread the rice thinly all over the nori
  4. add salmon and cucumber
  5. roll the nori
